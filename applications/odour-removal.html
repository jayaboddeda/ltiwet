Verification result

There has been no inspection voyage actually made ever since “ LTIWET-12 model” of the urinary prevention device was installed and started to run. Yet, the engine of the vessel had been working during the stoppage of the vessel, and it has been found after such working of the engine that there have been decrease of urinary scale with its increased softness on the drainpipe and increased ammonia odor. Irrespective of the increased ammonia odor, ammonia odor did not spread over during the removal of the toilet drainpipe. It was seen that there was no scale increase on the drainpipe.

Softening and removal of scale was found by the fact that softened scale was adhered to the screwdriver when such scale was rubbed by the screwdriver, and with more firm rubbing by the screwdriver, scale at the bottom of the drainpipe was also removed.

This device can remove scale and prevent its further formation with the reduction of ammonia odor by flushing water while less use of the toilet would lead to the increase of yellow tint on the western style toilet.

Periodical flushing of water could reduce yellow tint on the toilet. (Function of the periodical flushing water is available in some men’s toilet)
