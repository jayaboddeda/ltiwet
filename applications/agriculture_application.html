Agriculture Applications

Various tests and reports have shown the tremendous benefits LTIWET brings to the farm. In almost all fruit and vegetables tests, yield increase from 12% to over 30% has been achieved. In addition to yield and health benefits, LTIWET water increases water infiltration into soil (up to 20%), reduces water consumption (from 12 to 30%), keeping mass and minerals in roots deeper and longer with less water. This means: Less water consumption + increased effectiveness of fertilizers & pesticides = LOWER COSTS.

Key Benefits
  • Increase Water Infiltration: Up to 20% reduced run-off and evaporation.
  • Reduce Water Consumption: From 12% to 30%, keeping mass and minerals in roots.
  • Soil Remediation: Increase in root mass/aeration flushing out salt buildup.
  • Soil Aeration: Increase in micro-organisms improving humus and structure.
