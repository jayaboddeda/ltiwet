Poultry Applications

Animal industry reports may benefits including 85% decreased odor (of fecal matter) in hog operations utilizing activated water for both cleaning and consumption. Results showed that after hogs drank activated water the grade raised approximately 2 points, antibiotics usage decreased 20% - 30%, and mortality rate decreased from 18 - 10% to 3%.

FEED EFFICIENCY AND IMPROVED DIGESTION

LTIWET water increases the solubility of water; within the animal this allows it to realize more nutritional value from its food as well as increasing the effectiveness of medications and supplements by up to 30%.

OVERALL HEALTH AND LOWER MORTALITY RATES

These factors which contribute to greater feed efficiency, better disease resistance, increased effectiveness of medications and supplements— generally improved overall good health—would logically show an improvement in fetal as well as generational mortality rate(reduced from 18% to 3% for ducks).
